Need a break from the world but don't know where to go? You can go to the Bahamas or the caribbean, but Seychelles is the vacation spot for you! It is located just in the Indian Ocean off the coast of East Africa. You will never get bored there is so much to do! It is one of the most romantic destinations, and you can't get better than this.

Things to Do!

  • A ride between ports on the inter-island ferry
  • A view from above on a helicopter ride over the islands
  • A stroll through Victoria’s Public Market
  • A tour the L’Union Estate and Copra Factory, a historic coconut plantation
  • Snorkeling
  • Meet the bird that saved an island
  • Fising trip

Fun Facts!

  • Prince William and Kate went to Seychelles on their honeymoon
  • The capital of Seychelles, Victoria, is the smallest capital in the world. You can explore the entire city on foot in less than a day
  • Victoria, the capital city of Seychelles, is the smallest capital in the whole world.
  • The area under cultivation is limited, and the country is heavily dependent on imported food
  • The country’s fish consumption ranks among the highest in the world. The fishing industry is being developed with French assistance
